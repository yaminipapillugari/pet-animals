Horses

Horses do sleep standing up! They sleep laying down too, but only for short times. Learn all about the sleeping habits of horses.

Horses also build an ecosystem's biodiversity. Grazing horses focus on grasses, which protects the growth of other plants, like flowers.

Cows

All “cows” are female. Males are called bulls or steer. Before having a calf for the very first time, a female is called heifer. Once she has her first calf, she becomes a cow.

Cattle combat global warming by restoring carbon to the soil. ... 85% of land in the U.S. that is grazed by cattle, cannot be used for farming.
